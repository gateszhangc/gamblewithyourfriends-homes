About the Game

Welcome to Jeff Booth's Paradise

A co-op chaos game about teamwork, greed, and bad decisions. Don't call it gambling — call it a team-building exercise.

An online 1-6 player co-op "casino crawler" where you and your friends share one bank account and one massive debt. Gamble, buy sketchy items, make terrible decisions, and climb an ominous casino tower to hit your loan shark's daily quota in 5 minutes — or face the consequences.

Each "day," you get 5 minutes inside the casino to earn as much cash as possible. The tower has 4 distinctly themed floors, each with its own games of chance, randomized every time you enter. Unlock higher floors as you get richer — and bolder.

It's not my money, it's our money. Coordinate with your friends to manage a shared bank account while you gamble your way out of debt… because one bad all-in can ruin the run for everyone.

How It Works

Tickets, Trades, and Terrible Ideas

Hit your quota, complete unique challenges, and earn Tickets. Spend them on sketchy items to bend the odds. But even if you do everything "right"... Lady Luck still has the final say.

5 Minutes Per Day

You and your friends have exactly 5 minutes each day inside the casino to earn as much as possible. Every second counts. Every bet matters.

🏦

Shared Bank Account

One account. One debt. One team. Coordinate your bets because a single bad all-in can wipe out everyone's progress. Trust is optional.

Complete challenges, hit quotas, or make sacrifices to earn Tickets. Trade them for items that stack the deck in your favor — temporarily.
